This humorous picture book is a tribute to a very special mini-poodle named Little Prince, and also to all dogs and pets. It depicts the life of a Royal Canine - a self-proclaimed Prince of Chicago. The book is intended for dog and pet lovers, ages 9-99 for kids, their parents and grandparents, as a unique Chicago souvenir or a great gift for everyone.
