The Golf Swing Manifesto is the culmination of my life long journey to find a way to teach the golf swing without focusing on how to move your body. It's predecessor, "Two Lessons," still remains useful and published for legal reasons, but after so many revisions through the years, I felt it better to start fresh. I've spent over 20,000 hours of my life examining swings of every caliber and the result is this 8 page manifesto. My idea is that if you know how to swing the club properly then why should anyone care what body parts you are using to do so? It's so simple. If you love the game of golf then this is a must read!
