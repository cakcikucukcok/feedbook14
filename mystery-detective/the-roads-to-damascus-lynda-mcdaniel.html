Abit Bradshaw is back! Four years after that fateful summer in A Life for a Life, Abit Bradshaw faces the biggest challenge of his life. When a family of con artists fleeces his school and casts suspicion on him, Abit, with the support of his friends Della Kincaid and Alex Covington, sets out to find them and get payback. He takes a life-changing journey from Washington, D.C., through the mountains of Virginia, and finally home again to Laurel Falls, N.C. Along the way, he encounters a slew of characters—from sinners to saints—who help him discover his rightful place in the world.
