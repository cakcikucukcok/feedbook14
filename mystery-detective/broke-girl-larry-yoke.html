We may find ourselves, from time to time, becoming disjointed by various circumstances in our lives causing an emotional upheaval where the hammer and chisel of life can chip away at our sanity and the very core of existence, to the point, where we emotionally fall apart. We all have our breaking point!

At the center of the story is Senior Detective Gloria Ramos from Palo Pinto County, Texas, who is strong in mind, body and spirit, but finds herself in life-changing situations beyond her control. She finds a fourteen-year-old boy hiding during a cartel drug raid, where a mass murder occurred. She bonds with the boy who soon runs away. Fearing for his life from cartel revenge, Gloria and her new partner, Ranger Bryce Carrel, find themselves chasing his trail down to Mex­ico encountering multiple life-altering ordeals including lies, deceit, murder, mayhem, and corruption. Beaten down to an emotional pulp, she does find redemption, in a place, far, far away.
