Newlyweds Mikayla and Jesse Daulton are living a fairytale life. Jesse is a retired United States Army Ranger working as a flight medic on a rescue helicopter, while Mikayla runs a small advertising business out of their home. Although they are madly in love, there is one thing missing from their private paradise: a child of their own.

Jesse, who is as noble as his profession, is determined to give his wife the desires of her heart. After being suspended from his job for not following protocol, he makes a life-altering decision that will unwittingly place both his and Mikaylas lives in jeopardy.

Love Leads the Way is a poignant story of love and devotion between an Army soldier and his wife as they embark on an unforgettable journey together that teaches the true meaning of family, heroism, and sacrifice.
