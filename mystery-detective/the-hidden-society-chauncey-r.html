Larson Western meets Julian Franks in a cabin in northwestern Illinois and is given two flash drives. One tells the story of a secret society of wealthy people that’s a thousand years old, and the other tells him how to expose the Society before it can gain power over the world. Larson investigates and meets Marajo Smith who has been waiting twenty years to expose the Society. Together they set out to find a spot in Nevada where the Society’s information is hidden and put it on the Internet. But the Society’s ruthless leader is aware of them and sends his best killers to find and kill them. Luck and Julian’s preparations are all they’ve got to depend upon if they are to be successful.
