Delightful stories that will make your child smile while they learn!

Sues Story time delights is a book that comes in different series. Each book comprise of five adorable stories about interests, activities and adventures.

Can you spot words beginning with same letters?

For younger children learning to read, Sues story time delights books contains a Spot the word page which has letters and words that can easily be identified from reading the stories. Its a fun way of getting your child to know words beginning with different letters. Also, there are flashcard booklets derived from the book stories, available at: www.suestorytimedelights.com; to help support younger children in

learning letters and words.

Sues story time delights books are strategically written to complement each other alphabetically and as they are useful learning tools for younger children, they are equally as beautiful for older children, for a fun delightful read full of lifes little adventures.
