"There was an almighty whooshing sound and she was swept right off her feet and straight up through the ceiling. It was as if a giant vacuum cleaner had just descended from the sky and sucked her up."

When Jess drinks a can of glowing fizzy pop, she gets the strangest wibbling feeling in her stomach. Little does she know she's about to get transported onto a spaceship and caught up in the adventure of a lifetime. Meanwhile, Yusilli the alien is stranded down on Earth, searching for a way home. How will their worlds collide?

Nothing exciting ever happens in the sleepy village where Jess lives, but this might just be it...

Sapec! is the first story in the Zharo series. It is the first book written by new indie author Milly Blackmore.
