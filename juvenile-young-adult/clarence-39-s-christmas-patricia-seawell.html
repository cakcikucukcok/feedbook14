Christmas! What a busy time of year. Shopping malls, baking cookies, Christmas decorations, Christmas pageants, wrapping packages. But wait! Just what is all this busyness about anyway? Lets face it, often the real meaning of Christmas, the birthday of Christ Jesus, gets lost under all the wrappings of the holiday itself. In Clarences Christmas, a camel named Clarence and a Christmas tree angel named Angelica work with the other ornaments to save the real meaning of Christmas for one family. Lets join them to focus on what the bells, lights, and holly are really about, baby Jesus in the hay.
