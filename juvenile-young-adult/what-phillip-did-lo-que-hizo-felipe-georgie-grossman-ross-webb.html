Phillip is a little boy with a lot to talk about; he has had a very busy day. At the end of every day, Phillip and his mother sit down together at bedtime and talk about his day. Phillip tells his mother all about the wonderful things hes done today.

He splashed in puddles, drew pictures, ate his lunch, ran with friends, flew a kite in the park, and talked to his grandmother on the phoneas well as lots of other things! Phillip remembers all the things that filled his day before drifting off to sleep.

What Phillip Did includes vivid illustrations and simple, easy-to-read sentences that younger children can follow and beginning readers can read themselves. Phillips day comes to life for everyone to enjoy. Children can practice reading in English and Spanish and share time with their parents at the end of the day.
