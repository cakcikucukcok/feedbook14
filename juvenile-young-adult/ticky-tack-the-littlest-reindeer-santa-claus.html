"Ticky Tack - The Littlest Reindeer" is a holiday gem! Ticky Tack is a small but determined reindeer wanting to make it a Merry Christmas! This Classic Christmas Comic will have your family enjoying your holiday season! When Santa forgets a gift to a disabled boy, Ticky Tack flies his first voyage to rescue the Christmas holiday! Ticky Tack and Shortbread, his elf friend, create a Happy Holiday for a child less fortunate! A sweet holiday story to share! Touch the screen and this classic comes to life! In this delightfully heartwarming Christmas story you will learn about Ticky Tack, a tiny reindeer, who helps Santa and Shortbread the Elf deliver a little disabled boy's present on Christmas Eve. It's such a fun and uplifting read, and the 40+ wonderfully cute cartoon illustrations are absolutely holiday adorable. "Ticky Tack - The Smallest Reindeer is Cartoonist Walt Kelly's original "Santa Claus Funnies" 1930's work. Walt Kelly worked as an artist for Walt Disney in the 1930's, and later Dell comics, and was creator of the famous Pogo comic strips. Children will love this book, and will love you for getting it for them. Enjoy this Classic Christmas Comic cartoon adventure reading with them!
