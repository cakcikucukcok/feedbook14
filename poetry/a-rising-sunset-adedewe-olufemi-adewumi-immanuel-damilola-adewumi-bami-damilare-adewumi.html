An anthology written by a Father, Femi Adewumi, and His 2 sons (Immanuel and Bami). Their mother, a lay critique & admirer of the arts, having read their poems over the years, decided it was time for an anthology. The title of the book ‘A Rising Sunset’ is illustrative of the fact that it transcends 2 generations. 

Written over decades The Poems address a vast array of Life’s issues. From practical to philosophical, spiritual to physical, General and all-encompassing to unique and specific; Issues which many face throughout their lifetimes and so would find easily relatable. ‘A Rising Sunset’ is a book for anyone who dares question... A book for anyone who dares dream.
