Every form of spirit that lives and that has ever lived has its own, but equally valid, perspective on the universe; and every role played is important to the tapestry of wisdom that is woven into lifes wonderful theme. This book is a collection of poems, each inspired by and reflecting on moments of interest along what appears to be, and often times feels like, a solo journey. These poems are the songs that, in emanating from the writers heart, have served to sustain the balance that has been necessary for purposeful motion and fulfillment. Read either as poems or songs, these writings seek nothing other than connectionfirst to the self and then to all others who journey and sing.
