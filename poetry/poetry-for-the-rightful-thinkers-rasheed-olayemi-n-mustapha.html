THE POEMS Rightful thinking paves the way for righteous doings. This is why Poetry for the Rightful Thinkers has been packaged to restore rectitude in the world. It is a thoroughly researched write-up that expatiates the unparalleled significance of moral correctness as a key to societal development. The book highlights that the multifarious problems bedeviling the nations of the world today are not irretrievable if and only if people can adhere strictly to correctitude. This literary test is a learning material with exercises to test the comprehension of the readers after each poem.
