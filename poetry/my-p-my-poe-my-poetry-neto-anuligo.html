My p, my poe, my poetry a collection of poems from different spheres of life some of which are of love, nature, religion, individuality, and are hilarious. The poems are wrapped in tones describable as softly, humorous, critical, inspirational and much more. This book structures the poem in an alphabetical order, rather than in categories. However, the nature of some of the poems can be known by its title. Some of the love poems collected in this book are first love, from the very first, dead love, the place of true love, and much more. Some on nature are the likes of earth, once upon a time and the tear drops of the sky. Poems like the two rats in my house , total crises are intended to be funny. Some poems on individuality are a life of my own, loneliness of the mind, oh frank. Many other poems cannot be perfectly categorized as one can be humorous and at the same time critical or lovely. The following poetic expressions, which are in-text in this book is found below: Poem: Marco Fabio Aneto I have all days of my life told great love stories in merriness but one - My own story Poem: My Letter of Praise to Deceit You are the chameleon of all chameleons Poem: My Wife - My Life The soft and canorous voice good morning darling now sounds like that of a lion roaring Poem: Once Upon A Time _ _ when men were relentless on the beauty of their nakedness and the nakedness of their beauty. My p, my poe, my poetry, is a poetic book drawn from self-inspiration and inspirations from various minds, anonymous teachings and words of wisdom. This book is intended to be fun reading, entertaining, a way to broaden the mind horizon and useful for mans relaxation.
