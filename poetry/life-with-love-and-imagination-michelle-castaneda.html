Castaeda begins by sharing a poignant glimpse into the life of the mother of an autistic son and her personal struggles as she learns to cope with the judgments of others and provide the best for him. As she moves through her challenges the subsequent realization that unconditional love forever fills the heart, Castenada offers a moving message for anyone whose life journey has not been easy.
