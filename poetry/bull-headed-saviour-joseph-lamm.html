A BOOK OF SOUL ENERGIES IN POEMS & LYRICS

That Bull Headed Saviour exists, writes Joseph Lamm, is a testament to grace. When all is taken away, when we reach the pouring out of our self, it is our redemption.

After completing the poetic narrative Andeans Ascent, Joseph Lamm found critical acclaim and modest chart success in music with the albums Watching The Coming Storm and Standing In The Tower.

In 2003 he began work on a project that was born out of a passion for the writings of William Blake. Lamm also had high esteem for beat poet, author, and playwright Michael McClures body of work, especially his bold and daring collection Rain Mirror. The result of an emotional and physiological collapse as McClure himself described it, Rain Mirror was a foreshadowing of Lamms own parallel experience two years later, as a purging of his soul gave way to a wondrous spiritual awakening. Bull Headed Saviour entwines the beauty of nature, the emotion and desires of the heart, and the spirit of the soul toward a common celebration found within everyone.

This collection contains many of the poems and lyrics that were written during this period, along with selected lyrics from the albums Watching The Coming Storm, Standing In The Tower, and his newest work, Hot As Sun, Vol.1.
