Angels, Trees, You and Meby Joseph Pio Asterita delves into the world of spiritual belief, into the belief that you are a magnificent aspect of God, of Spirit, needing only to be reminded of this to start you on your realization journey.Here is an excerpt of the book that tells you of your innate spiritual nature:

Gods Poem is you

You are Gods poetry

Gods ultimate creation

A symphony in motion

A sweet melody of

Muscle, bones, spirit and heart

We have helpers waiting to assist us to discover our inner sanctity.The poems about Angels and Trees tell us about the aid that is ours for the asking. These poems herald our state of inner peace and the wonderful world of nature that is ours to enjoy. Every tree has its own identity. Native Americans rightly called them the tree people because they experienced the subtle energy and wisdom that came from trees.That is why great comfort can come from being around trees.And there are angels, too.Created to show us the way and be our spiritual companions. All of this beauty has been put in place to remind us of who we are.We just have to be open to it.

So it is all here for us, our true identity and the help to discover it.Read this book slowly, absorb it, keep it in your pocket, purse or carry bag. Read it in your spare moments to get a lift and insight into your self. Or take time during your day just for you and digest a poem or two that tell of the wonder that is you.
