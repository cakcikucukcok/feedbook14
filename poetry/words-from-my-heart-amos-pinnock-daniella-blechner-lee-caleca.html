Above all put on love, for love is the bond of perfection. 3:14

 

Yes, I am a guy, and I do have emotions. My heart is on my sleeve and yes, I am what you call a "Hopeless Romantic." Plus you can say I'm more emotional than the average guy and yes I'll happily embrace that because I am. And with all these emotions raring inside of me, I decided to write.

 

So here's some words from my heart, things I've imagined doing with the love of my life, words from when my heart got broken, letters to my future wife and other scattered thoughts that lingered in my heart.

 

As much as these are words are from my heart, I'm sure these words have been spoken in your heart too.

Yes, at times love can bring pain, but it is also a beautiful thing to experience.

 

I started this book in pain and finished it through love.
