The book was written in accordance to the reflections of the author. The authors intention in writing this book entitled Reflecting on the Footprints of the Master is to guide true seekers to a path that would begin their spiritual journey through his footprints, which guides them to walk the path and step on the rocks that he stumble upon. The book not only comprises of a birds eye view on the 6 Prong Mind Profiling Method, which would comfort one to the understanding of the mind in a simple way but also to comprehend the characteristics of a person, a method which the author conceptualized and developed. The book also gives a vivid explanation of areas to focus while disembarking on the Spirituals Journey.
