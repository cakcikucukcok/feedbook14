DETROIT PISTONS Chaplain of 22 Years

HANDLING LIFE’S HARDSHIPS offers you insights on what to do when you have obeyed God, but still find yourself asking, “Where is He?” You will learn what to do when your faithfulness seems to be rewarded with adversity, confusion and difficulty.

Robert Joyce reveals God’s thoughts about personal hardship and answers questions that will help you in your quest to understand and persevere when things don’t turn out as you have expected. He explains how to process your experiences according to God’s Word. Rather than panic, you’ll understand the process God uses to develop character and integrity in our lives.

This book will activate your faith and encourage you to fulfill God’s purpose for your life.
