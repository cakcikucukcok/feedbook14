It is the best of many literary worlds! Author Kenneth Terry writes masterfully in a style that makes a 1st Century book come alive with 21st Century relevance! Presiding Bishop Ronald D. Kenneth Terrys book, A MODEL FOR ALL BELIEVERS, a study of First Thessalonians, is a practical study of the epistle for pastors, teachers and believers.
