Do you feel like your lawn-care company is running you? Does it seem no matter how much money you make you have to turn right around and spend it to keep your doors open? I know how you feel! I beat my head against a wall for many years as my lawn care company began to grow until I finally put all the pieces to the puzzle together. My goal is to help you get over the hump much faster than I did and save you a bunch of money along the way. This book contains every bit of knowledge I gained over the years by learning from my own mistakes. Each chapter covers various aspects of the business with action steps after each one. If you believe you can build a successful business, I do too, and I sincerely hope this book helps you reach your goals in business and in life.
