14 Reasons Youre Not Letting Yourself Heal is packed with invaluable new insight and life-changing perspective crucial for turning the pain and misery of devastating grief around. Page by page, the reader is freed from the agonizing and emotionally debilitating stranglehold of grief and sorrow. By exposing fourteen widespread psychologically harmful myths, 14 Reasons decisively debunks each. For survivors trapped in the thrall of these myths, the criticality cannot be overstated. Throughout D. Anthonys experience as a grief recovery coach, these myths have proven responsible for some of the deepest agony and biggest deterrents to recovery. Worst yet, they mangle psyches, emotions, and decisionssabotaging any impetus or motivation mustered up along the way. Hiding in utter anonymity, they silently plague many. That is, until now. If its time to discover a new path no longer consumed by misery and pain and finally reclaim a healthier, more hopeful life, 14 Reasons Youre Not Letting Yourself Heal is definitely the book for you!
