Exposed: How To Make Women Fall In Love With You Fast

A lot of my clients ask me the question ‘how do you make a woman fall in love with you?’ My normal response is ‘why would a woman want to fall in love with you?’ My clients will come back with answers like ‘I have a brand new BMW,’ or ‘I run a very successful company,’ or ‘I make a lot of money,’ in other words they tell me about possessions they own, unfortunately these are not what most women need to fall in love with a man. If you really want to know how to make a woman fall in love with you, I think you may be in for a shock.

Before we go any further, I have written this book for guys who are currently single or have just started dating a new girl and want to know how to make her fall in love with them.

I have been in the dating industry and coaching guys to have more success with women for a very long time now. Over the years I have seen a pattern with the guys…

It seems there are three groups of guys out there. Often, it is very easy for me to spot which group a guy belongs to.

The first group of guys are normally defeated before they begin. This group of guys believe that you have to be tall, good looking, have lots of possessions and be rich if you want a beautiful girl to fall in love with you.

In others words they have no real understanding of what it is that women really look for in a guy.

The second group of guys have worked very hard on their career and have become very successful in their chosen field. They still fail with women

The next group of guys are what most people call the naturals with women. They are the envy of the other two groups.
