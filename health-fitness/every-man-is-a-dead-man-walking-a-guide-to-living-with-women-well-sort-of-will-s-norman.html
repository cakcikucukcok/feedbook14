Hello I’m Will,

And I’ve written a book for men, all about women…all the things that I think they need to know. However there is a problem - like a lot of men I started out clueless, and never really improved from there.

I speak as the authority…the authority on failing miserably with women for most of my life – if failing with women was an education, I’d have a PHD in it.

All that said however, one day I found myself sort of on the shelf, and to my complete surprise some women took an interest in me. From this point onwards I set about trying to understand why.

I learned very little, but what I did learn people (women especially) kept telling me I should write in a book, so here it is, “Every Man is a Dead Man Walking, a guide to living with women…well sort of…” written with great care and attention by yours truly.

So if you're a typical man who's clueless about women, or a woman who wants an insight into the mind of a man, then read this book...my reviewers say you'll learn something and apparently you'll laugh in the process.
