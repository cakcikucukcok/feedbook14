The One is an intimate collection of stories about relationships and the journey to understanding their impact on how we love. As unique as the impression of a fingerprint, all women have had a unique experience that has left an indelible mark on their heart. Whether it was the lover they thought they'd spend the rest of their lives with, the father who was either emotionally and/or physically absent, or the seemingly unanswered prayers from our Father in Heaven, we've all had relationships that have defined and redefined who we are and how we love.

Through personal testaments and prompted reflections, this book will help you explore love as a resource in order to leverage it to the benefit of yourself and those around you. Discover the truth about why you love the way you do so that you can more passionately and effectively achieve God's will in your life. The perfect addition to book club meetings and girls' nights out, The One spurs relevant, engaging dialogue to create communities of meaningful support and enrichment.
