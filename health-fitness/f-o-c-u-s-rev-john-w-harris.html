F.O.C.U.S. is connecting the people of God to the will of God! Mankind is constantly looking to find their purpose in life. The Word of God teaches us that it happens in accordance to Mathew 6:33: But seek ye first the kingdom of God, and his righteousness; and all these things shall be added unto you. When we learn to focus on the things of God and not our circumstances, it gives us access to greater in the kingdom of God by placing us in line for our but God moment. A but God moment is defined as God taking what seems like an impossible situation in our lives, despite what seems to be insurmountable odds, and making the impossible possible in even the most adverse conditions. All God desires is to bless us. All we have to do is F.O.C.U.S. Hes waiting.
