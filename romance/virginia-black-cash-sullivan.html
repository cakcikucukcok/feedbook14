women coveted Michael Brown, Karis

high school sweetheart, a former war-hero

and a dedicated Blacksburg police offi cer.

He used and abused that knowledge to his

advantage. Though outward appearances

epitomized a wholesome family, living the

American dream, behind closed doors, the

dream had become a nightmare. Hopelessly,

Kari learned to withstand the mental and

physical abuse of a man who left for Iraq as a

young and loving husband and returned as a

stranger full of rage and unpredictability. In

Winstons devoted protection gives Kari

journey from the dark prison she has been

captive to, into the light of a new, more

hopeful future.
